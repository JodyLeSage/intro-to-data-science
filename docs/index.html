## An Introduction to Data Science with Scikit Learn by Jody LeSage
## What is data science? Note: Data science is an overloaded term. Depending on who you talk to a "data scientist" can be anywhere from a person who is good with Excel all the way up to an engineer that designs and manages massively parallel computations. I prefer to view data science as an interdisciplinary field that is focused on extracting knowledge (something that has meaning) from data (doesn't have explicit meaning)

## The Classification Problem Note: There are many types of problems you can solve with data science, but this talk is only going to focus on one: the classification problem. For those of you who are not already familiar with it, you're essentially trying to predict which label should be applied to a piece of data based on other piece of data you've seen in the past. The classic example is, of course, image recognition. You show an angorithm 1000 pictures of cats and 1000 pictures of dogs - this is called the learning phase. Then you take a new picture of an animal and you ask your algorithm "is this a picture of a cat or a dog?" - this is called the prediction phase. And you hope your algorithm can label the pictures.
